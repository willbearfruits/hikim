Working surface

Built like a DAW, bent toward damage.

  • Audio and MIDI timeline editing
  • Session grid launching and scene follow
  • TEETH rack macros and MIDI learn
  • WIRES device and instrument modes
  • VST3, AU and LV2 hosting paths
  • Built-in RUST, GRAVEL and HYMN instruments
  • Patch modulation from LFO, chaos and follower sources
  • ffmpeg-backed media decode fallback
